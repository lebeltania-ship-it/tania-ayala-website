Maximize Your Results

After-Treatment Care

Follow these simple guidelines after each session to get the best results and help your body heal and sculpt faster.

Drink Plenty of Water

Drink at least 8–10 glasses of water after every session. Hydration is essential for flushing the toxins and fat your body releases during treatment. Herbal teas and coconut water also count — avoid alcohol for 24 hours.

Wear Your Faja or Waist Trainer

Put on your faja or waist trainer immediately after each session and wear it consistently — ideally 6–8 hours per day. Compression holds the tissue in its new shape, accelerates lymphatic flow, and dramatically improves your sculpting results.

Stay Consistent

Results build session by session. Avoid heavy meals, excess sodium, and alcohol for the first 24 hours after treatment. Light walking or gentle movement helps stimulate lymphatic drainage. The more consistent you are, the more dramatic your transformation.

Results Disclaimer: Individual results may vary. The number of sessions needed depends on your body type, lifestyle, and goals. Results are enhanced by proper hydration, consistent use of compression garments, and following all post-treatment guidance. Our treatments are not a substitute for medical care.
