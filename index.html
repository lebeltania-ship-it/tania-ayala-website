About Us

The Art of Wood Body Sculpting

At Tania Ayala Fajas Colombianas, we specialize in maderotherapy — an ancient Colombian technique using hand-carved wooden tools to reshape, firm, and contour the body without surgery.

Our treatments target cellulite, improve lymphatic drainage, break down fatty deposits, and sculpt your natural curves. Combined with authentic fajas colombianas, we deliver visible, lasting results session after session.
